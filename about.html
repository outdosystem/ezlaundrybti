Our story

Seven years of caring for
the finest wardrobes in Bathinda.

From two German washers in a small unit to forty franchise partners across Punjab — ez Laundry has always been a family-run house obsessed with the smallest details of care.

The beginning

A quiet obsession with fabric.

In 2018, a small unit in Bathinda opened its doors with two German front-loaders and a promise: no fabric would ever leave shrunk, dulled, or mistreated. That promise still runs every machine in every ez Laundry unit today.

We built our workflow the way a five-star hotel runs its laundry room: sort meticulously, dose gently, press with patience, and finish with a fragrance you'll remember. It's slower. It's more expensive. And it's the only way we know.

Our mission

To care for every fibre that comes through our doors like it's the last of its kind — with the patience of a craftsman and the precision of a laboratory.

Our vision

To become Punjab's most trusted premium fabric-care house by 2028 — recognised for our chemistry, our craft and the small daily luxuries we deliver.
