FAQ

Frequently asked

Everything you'd want to know before your first pickup.

Ask a question
How does pickup and delivery work?
Book a slot via WhatsApp, phone or our website. A uniformed valet arrives with a monogrammed garment bag, notes any special care instructions, and hands you a digital receipt. We return your order within your chosen window.
What if my garment is damaged during cleaning?
Every garment is inspected before and after cleaning. We carry full insurance on your order and will replace or refund up to 10x the service value for any verified damage — no arguments.
Do you use eco-friendly products?
Yes. Our detergents are biodegradable, our dry-cleaning solvent is hydrocarbon-based (not perc), and our German machines use up to 60% less water than industry standard.
Which areas do you serve?
We currently service all major sectors of Bathinda with same-day pickup. Express delivery is available in the central zones, and franchise expansion is under way for Punjab tier-2 cities.
Can I schedule a recurring weekly pickup?
Absolutely — most of our members are on our weekly 'Set & Forget' plan. Pick a day and slot, and we'll be there like clockwork.
